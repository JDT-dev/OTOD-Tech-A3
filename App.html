'PUPS & KIDS'

Why do you need it?

Help for your puppy and family. It's obvious that parents need guidance with training a new puppy while balancing the needs of their family. Encouraging parents to schedule and focus on the puppy’s needs during the first 16 weeks (“Critical period"), will help create a strong foundation for when the puppy grows into an adult dog. By focusing on the interaction between a puppy and a child, this will help the parent and child realise that they have an affectionate, caring lifetime companion that truly is humanity's best friend.

Our Motivation

We all need to be more responsible.

Research conducted in 2012-2013 discovered that approximately 211,655 dogs end up in pounds, and of that, approximately 43,900 are killed (21%). Dogs are loving, compassionate sentient beings that live in the moment and give nothing but unconditional love. We should not be killing so many innocent creatures. It's not the dog's fault that they end up in that situation. It's ours due to our lack of knowledge around what a puppy needs early on in their life.

That's our motivation, to equip families with the knowledge to prevent future deaths of dogs because of the lack of socialisation and training received during their formative years.

Features

Puppy Calendar & Scheduler

  • A tailored 16-week socialisation plan that is automatically broken down into step-by-step daily tasks and scheduled via the calendar. Each task will be adjustable according to the weather requirements to lessen chances of days missed.

  • Daily push notifications to the parent's phone to remind them of the daily socialisation requirements.

Pro Training Tips & Guides

  • Progress achievements rewarded in the form of completion badges to keep parents and children engaged due to feeling a sense of accomplishment.

  • Video demonstrations of how to socialise a puppy in-house and in other environments.

Social Network For Your Puppy

Help your Puppy Network!

  • Social networking feature to connect parents with other families to provide a support network and ability to organise puppy parties; to help socialise their puppies in a group environment.
